Our Story

A Family Kitchen Since 2005

Bella Vista started as a tiny trattoria with six tables. Today we're a beloved dining destination, but our soul hasn't changed: fresh ingredients, slow-cooked sauces, and recipes passed down from Nonna Lucia herself.

  • Handmade pasta, made fresh daily
  • Imported Italian olive oil & cheeses
  • Wood-fired oven, built by hand
  • Family-owned and operated

Why Diners Love Us
